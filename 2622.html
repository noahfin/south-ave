Description

The 6,060-sq. ft. two story building is for sale at a price of $750,000. The ownership will also consider a long-term net lease of the building as well. The first floor is renovated into quality professional offices that will make a high impact on clients and customers. It has large lobby, leading in from a beautiful double door glass entrance. Located in the heart of downtown Wappingers Falls, next to the police station and a two minute drive to Route 9. The building is a 7-minute drive to New Hamburg Train Station (Hudson Line) and about a 28 minute drive to Stewart International Airport. The upstairs modern office space has a large conference room with several large offices. The second floor could be used as additional space or be leased out to another tenant. Parking is in the front, side and rear of the property as well on the street directly across from the building.
